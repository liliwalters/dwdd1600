Digital citizenship

What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship is the responsible, ethical, and safe use of technology and online spaces, involving respectful behavior, digital literacy, awareness of one's online footprint, and understanding rights and responsibilities in the digital world

What is Digital Law?

digital law refers to what you are and are not allowed to do while using the Internet

What is AUP?

Acceptable Use Policy, a set of rules defining permitted and prohibited uses of an organization's network, devices, and data, ensuring security, compliance, and responsible behavior

What is plagiarism?

Plagiarism is the act of using another person's words, ideas, or original work without providing proper credit

What is academic dishonesty?

Academic Dishonesty is any deceptive action used to gain an unfair academic advantage or misrepresent original work.

What are the rules for AI Generated Content?

the rules for AI-generated content are structured around transparency, accountability, and safety. Whether in school, at work, or in public spaces, the core requirement is that users must disclose when AI has been used and remain responsible for the final output.
