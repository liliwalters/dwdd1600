The Committee Referral

Once introduced, the bill is referred to the appropriate standing committee (or multiple committees) based on its subject matter. For example, a health care bill might go to the Committee on Energy and Commerce. The committee leadership decides whether the bill even receives a hearing, this is called a markup session. The majority of bills die here, never moving forward.
