The Chamber Vote

After debate, the full chamber votes on the bill. There are three voting methods:

  • Voice Vote - Members call out "yea" or "nay."
  • Division Vote - Members stand to be counted.
  • Recorded Vote - Each member's vote is officially recorded (most common for major bills).

A simple majority (218 of 435 in the House; 51 of 100 in the Senate) is needed to pass.
