The Presidential Action

The bill is enrolled and sent to the President, who has four options

  1. sign the bill - it becomes a law immediately
  2. veto the bill - it is returned to Congress with objections
  3. do nothing (Congress in session) - after 10 days, it becomes law automatically
  4. do nothing (Congress adjourned) - after 10 days it is killed, this is a "pocket veto"
